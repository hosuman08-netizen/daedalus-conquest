## 1. Ancient Foundations (Solid Genetics + Archaeology)

### Timeline & IVC (Pre-Harappan to Decline)
- **~7000–3300 BCE (Pre-Harappan/Neolithic)**: Mehrgarh (Balochistan) agriculture/domestication/trade foundations. Gradual proto-urban.
- **~3300–2600 BCE (Early Harappan)**: Regional cultures, proto-urban across Indus/Ghaggar-Hakra.
- **~2600–1900 BCE (Mature Harappan/IVC peak)**: Urban standardization (weights, undeciphered script, grid planning, advanced drainage, baked brick). No palaces/temples evident. Trade to Mesopotamia/Oman. Pop 1-5M est. Sites: Rakhigarhi (largest), Mohenjo-daro (Great Bath), Harappa, Dholavira (reservoirs), Lothal (port). Mystery + mystery script for lore.
- **~1900–1300 BCE (Late/Decline)**: De-urbanization — aridification, monsoon/river shifts (Ghaggar-Hakra/Saraswati drying). No large-scale invasion evidence. Local continuity + regional variation.
- **~1500/1750–1000 BCE (Early Vedic)**: Indo-Aryan gradual migration/diffusion post-IVC (steppe-related post-2000 BCE). Rigveda composition. Pastoral/semi-nomadic in Punjab.
- **~1000–500 BCE (Later Vedic)**: Eastward to Ganges, iron/PGW culture, kingdoms (Kuru-Panchala), Upanishads (ātman-Brahman, early karma/samsara).
- **~500 BCE+**: Epics bridge Vedic ritual to narrative/devotional. Second urbanization, Mahajanapadas.

**Rakhigarhi (Haryana)**: Mature ~2800-2300 BCE. 2024 Mound 7: 56 skeletons. Ongoing 2026. DNA 2019 (Shinde Cell): Iranian-related (pre-farming) + AASI (Andaman-like), **zero Steppe**. "Indus Periphery Cline". Primary modern SA source. 2025: Sequeira/Koraga "Proto-Dravidian" ~2400 BCE component (possible IVC overlap). 2026 discussions reinforce continuity. "Braided stream" (IVC core + post-1500 BCE steppe 10-30% + AASI).

No "invasion". Steppe MLBA post-IVC (~1500 BCE Swat).

**Recent sites 2025-26**: Lothal resumed 2025-26 (tiny gold particles, unicorn seals, bead-making drill bits/earnestite, furnaces, fish otoliths/bones, cultural layers — post-Mature Harappan continuity, merged with Sorath traditions, maritime strength; high-tech drones/digital mapping for NMHC museum). Dholavira heritage. New Harappan Ratadiya Ri Dheri (Rajasthan Thar 2025). Sanauli (UP ~2000-1800 BCE): solid-wheel carts (debate chariot vs ox-cart). Elephanta ~1500yo stepped reservoir. Vallabhipur etc.

**Game use (solid)**: Indigenous urban sophistication + admixture roots. Heroes as "Echoes" of deep civilizational layers. IVC as "lost golden age" (planned cities, sanitation, mystery script/unicorn seals = ancient secrets/tech). Climate/river decline + new arrivals = FOMO/scarcity drama. Genetic mixing (Iranian+AASI+Steppe) for bloodlines/factions. Sanauli carts for ancient war tech lore.

### Tamil Nadu / South India Early Developments
- Sivagalai (near Adichanallur, Thamirabarani): TNSDA 2025 report (Rajan & Sivanantham). AMS (Beta Analytic) charcoal/iron context: iron artifacts ~2953–3345 BCE (some ~5,300+ ya claims). Knives, axes, hooks + paddy husks ~1155 BCE. "Iron Age in South while North Copper?" — reported BBC/Frontline 2025.
- Keezhadi (Sangam): ~580 BCE+ (Beta AMS), brick structures, Tamil-Brahmi potsherds (~685 BCE some claims), iron, trade. 10 phases. 2025-26: ASI internal critique of early report (ambiguous/incomplete); excavator rejection Feb 2026. Political/cultural framing (Tamil pride).
- **Solid**: Iron use in TN by mid-1st millennium BCE (or earlier per claims); Sangam urban/literate culture with literacy indicators.
- **Debated**: Extreme 4th millennium BCE iron dates (context/calibration needs replication; not mainstream consensus yet). Keezhadi chronology under review.
- **Game use**: South Indian depth + Dravidian continuity as balance to North epics. Pride hook for diverse 20-30s.

### Epics (Ramayana, Mahabharata) as Moral Frameworks
- **Itihasa**: "Thus it happened" — cultural memory + ethical teaching, not strict documentary history (scholarly consensus). Oral traditions compiled over time, mythologized real polities/wars.
- Composition: Ramayana core ~7th–5th/500 BCE (pre-Buddhist layers). Mahabharata layers ~4th BCE–4th CE (core earlier Kuru conflicts ~1000-800 BCE). Narrative: Ramayana precedes Mahabharata. PGW culture at Hastinapur/Kurukshetra links material but scale/dates debated. Dwarka submerged Harappan-era (debated).
- Core: From Vedic ṛta (cosmic order) → **Dharma** (contextual/svadharma duty, moral gray areas — Gita dilemmas, Yudhishthira choices). **Karma** (action-consequence-rebirth/samsara, Upanishads onward; dramatized in war as consequence). purusharthas. Ramayana ideal kingship/virtue (Vishnu avatar); Mahabharata tragic complexity.
- Archaeology: Cultural layers at epic sites but no proof of epic-scale war. Geography plausible for Iron Age north India.
- Modern role: Living moral guides, festivals (Diwali Rama return, Holi Krishna), identity. 2025-26: ethical/leadership value emphasized.
- **Game use (perfect)**: Prestige = samsara/rebirth cycle (ascend with karma multiplier). Heroes as "Echoes" (fictional archetypes: Krishna strategist, Rama dharma-king, Arjuna duty-warrior, Draupadi resilience, Durga protector, Hanuman loyalty, Ganesha remover, Shiva creator/destroyer). Clan/family duty. No direct names in public to avoid issues — internal mapping only.
