IVC profile = primary source population for modern South Asians. "Indus Periphery Cline".
- 2025 updates: Sequeira et al. (2025) proposes additional "Proto-Dravidian" ancestry component (~4400 ya, ~2400 BCE branching) in IVC-era mixes. "Braided stream" model: 3+ pillars (IVC core + later steppe input post-1500 BCE 10-30% in some groups + AASI). Continuity strong in NW groups.
